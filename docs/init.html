Init of engine

Firstly you need to initialize SDL2 and SDL2_Mixer.
Then you need to initialize SDL2_renderer and SDL_Window.
Then you need to call engine.getSDL2data(renderer) where renderer - SDL_Renderer initialied before.
Then you can initialize texture atlas with call to engine.loadTextureAtlas ("path/to/file"). Argument - path to texture file in png.
Also you can initialize sounds for death and level end with call to engine. loadSounds("death", "", "end"). Where death - path to death sound in .wav, second argument is unused, end - path to level end sound in .wav.
Also you can add your custom init, specialized for your game or for your modification of engine.
